Avocado toast

description

Toasted bread topped with mashed avocado light seasoning.

ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 1 avocado
  • 1/2 of a lemon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper

steps

  1. Toast bread using either toaster or toaster oven.
  2. Cut avocado into halves and remove pit.
  3. Juice the lemon.
  4. Mash together the avocado halves, salt, pepper, and lemon juice inside of a bowl.
  5. Spread the mixture onto the toast with a butter knife.
