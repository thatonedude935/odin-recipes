guacamole

description

A dip made from avocado, onions, tomatoes,cilantro, and lime juice.

ingredients

  • 3 mashed avocados
  • 2 diced tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons of chopped cilantro
  • 1/2 a cup of diced onions
  • 1 teaspoon of minced garlic
  • 1 juiced lime
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

steps

  1. Mix ingredients together in medium bowl.
  2. Either serve immediately or store in airtight container then refridgerate.
